Flip Bottle 3d

Project Info:

You have to flip a plastic bottle in the exciting arcade game Flip Bottle 3D. Make the bottle jump or even double flip. How? Just tap the screen at the right moment and jump!

Feature
  • In-app purchase
  • Banner & Interstitial ads
  • Firebase Notification
  • Firebase Remote Config : controls game function by cloud
Technology
  • Unity3d
  • Firebase (Notification, Remote Config)
  • Admob
